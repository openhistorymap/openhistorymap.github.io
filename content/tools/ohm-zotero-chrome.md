A small Chrome extension that lives next to the Zotero Connector and
fills a specific gap: attaching arbitrary web pages as *linked* URL
attachments to an item that already exists in your library.

Used together with the OHM Zotero plugin, it turns the everyday research
browser into a one-click pipeline for enriching OHM source records.
