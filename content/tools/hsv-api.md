Photography only goes back to the late 18th century, but humans have
been making located views of the world for far longer. The Historical
Street View project collects those views and lets you walk through them
on a map, choosing both *where* and *when* you stand.

`hsv-api` is the back-end that serves those located views;
[visions.ofthepast.org](https://visions.ofthepast.org) is the public
front-end built on top of it.
