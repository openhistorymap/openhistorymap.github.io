GTFS describes today's transit networks; HTFS is the equivalent draft
for *historical* ones, where every line, stop and schedule comes with an
explicit time-of-validity so a network can be reconstructed for any
chosen date.

The repository is archived but the design is the basis for ongoing work
on transit layers inside OHM, and the spec is the right starting point
for anyone modelling historical mobility data.
