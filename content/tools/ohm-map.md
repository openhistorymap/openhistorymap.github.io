The OHM web client is the canonical entry point to the project's data:
a MapLibre-based interface that lets you pan and zoom across geography
*and* across time, switching between historical layers, base maps and
thematic overlays.

It is the public face of the platform; the rest of the stack on this page
exists either to feed it data, or to extend the workflow around it.
